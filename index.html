How to play
  • 🎮 Move with arrow keys, WASD, or touch/drag on iPad

  • 🧠 A math problem shows up with two answer eggs. Eat the right one to LEVEL UP!

  • 🔥 Power — eat ANY dragon, even big ones (8 seconds)
  • ⭐ Level Up — grow extra big right away
